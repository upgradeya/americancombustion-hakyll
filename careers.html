Employment Opportunities

American Combustion is in the primary business of providing the metals industry with the highest standard of combustion technology in the shortest turnaround time possible. Currently, our global offer includes designing, fabricating, and commissioning equipment as well as optimizing the energy usage for the customer's process. The future of ACI lies in becoming internationally recognized as a major innovator in combustion technology. In addition to maintaining our standards for Know-How, Reliability, Honesty and Safety as we grow, we will assume the responsibility of becoming a learning center for the advancement of technology in our areas of expertise.

American Combustion Culture

The engineering culture at ACI is open, direct, and informal. We are goal and achievement oriented. We value the needs and results of the individual. We do not pursue personal gain at the expense of group accomplishment or company growth.
